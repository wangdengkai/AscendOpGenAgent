# E30005 Device_Connection_Failure

**页面ID:** atlaserrorcode_15_0129  
**来源:** https://www.hiascend.com/document/detail/zh/CANNCommunityEdition/850/maintenref/troubleshooting/atlaserrorcode_15_0129.html

---

#### Symptom

Failed to connect the device.

#### Possible Cause

1. Heartbeats are missing on the device.
2. The underlying channel is disconnected.

#### Solution

1. Obtain the device log and contact technical support at https://www.hiascend.com/support.
2. Restart the device.
